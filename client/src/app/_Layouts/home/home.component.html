Write

Have a thought or idea? Jot it down in our portable, secure and dynamic platform. JOT makes it easy to write about your life and emotions. It's a private diary, a place to express yourself, or simply a place where you can write whatever you please.

Share

While you can always keep your entries private, some thoughts are worth sharing to the world. Make a journal public so the JOT community can view it. Unlike other platforms, you decide what information you want to share.

Explore

See what other people are thinking and feeling as others publish their personal journal entries. Feeling angry or happy and want to see if others can relate? Use JOT's emoji search to find entries that fit your mood. If you like someone's writing, follow them to see what they'll write next!
